I'm trying to download a video from a streaming website.

I've found out the video is segmented with MPEG-DASH format. So, I could find a .mpd file, init.mp4 files for video and audio each, and seg-*.m4s files for video and audio each.

How can I combine these files into the original video file?

I've downloaded all the segment m4s files with chrome extension mass downloader but can't find a way of either convert the init.mp4 to m4s so that I can merge it with the other seg-.m4s files; OR convert the seg-.m4s files into .mp4 (what I've already tried using ffmpeg, but it seems not be working since ffmpeg needs the header, which is the init.mp4 itself)

What should I do?

Note: I don't know NOTHING about js, ajax, html. So, who answers this, will have to do in laymen words.
